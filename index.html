Course Details

賽道詳情

Start to Aid Station 1 (Powderface Ridge Trailhead)

起點至補給站1 (Powderface Ridge Trailhead)

15 km • 500m elevation gain

Gentle climb through forest trails with some rocky sections, descending to the plains and follow trail back to trailhead.

穿過森林小徑的緩坡，小部分碎石路段，最後下降至平原。

Easy 簡單

Aid Station 1 to 2 (Elbow Fall Parking Lot)

補給站1至2 (Elbow Fall Parking)

14 km • 680m elevation gain

Steep climb with switchback.

陡峭的爬升。

Difficult 困難

Aid Station 2 to 3 (Moose Mountain Trailhead Parking)

補給站2至3 (Moose Mountain Trailhead Parking)

10.5 km • 600m elevation gain

Climb on well paved road and trail to last aid station.

爬上鋪好的道路和步道至最後一個補給站。

Moderate 中等困難

Aid Station 3 to Finish (Allen Bill Day Use Area)

補給站3至終點 (Allen Bill Day Use Area)

14.5 km • 170m elevation gain • 750m elevation loss

Flowing single-track through forest trails down with some climbs.

穿越森林小徑下坡，有些爬升。

Moderate 中等困難
