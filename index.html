Important Notes

重要事項

This is a self-supported challenge, not an organized race

呢個係自主挑戰，唔係正式比賽

Challengers are responsible for their own safety and personal equipment

挑戰者需自行負責安全同個人裝備

Teams of exactly 4 members must start and finish together (3-member teams can register but do not qualify as finishing teams)

4人團隊必須一齊起步同完成挑戰（三人團隊可以報名，但唔符合資格成為完成團隊）

If one team member cannot complete the challenge, the 3-member team can continue to finish as survivors but does not qualify as a finishing team, while a 2-member team must abandon the challenge.

如團隊其中一員未能完成挑戰，三人團隊可以繼續完成挑戰成為生還者，但唔符合資格成為完成團隊；二人團隊則必須放棄挑戰。

All team members need to record the challenge with any watch/Strava on their own

每位隊員要各自用任何手錶或Strava記錄全程挑戰

Basic aid stations provided by volunteers (with water/snacks/hot meals/first aid kit)

義工會提供基本補給站（可能包括水/小食/少量熱食/急救包）

Take photos at checkpoints and share on social media with #SaanKaaLaa

喺檢查點影相並喺社交媒體分享 #SaanKaaLaa
